About Me

Education & Experience

I hold a Master's degree in Translation Studies from a prestigious university and have over 10 years of experience in the translation industry. My academic background provided a strong foundation in translation theory and practice, enhancing my linguistic skills and cultural understanding. Professionally, I've worked with a diverse range of clients, including multinational corporations, legal firms, publishing houses, and individual authors. My experience spans legal document translation, marketing material localization, technical manual translation, and literary translation. I am dedicated to bridging language gaps and ensuring effective communication across cultures. My commitment to quality and precision has earned me a reputation for delivering accurate and culturally appropriate translations. Additionally, I am an active member of several translation associations and continuously seek to improve my skills through professional development.
